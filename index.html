Welcome to Prismatic Pink! This website is intended to be a compendium of everything I enjoy and cherish: my collections, the media I consume, and the art I create.

All my content is 100% SFW, except for some written reviews on media logs that may discuss mature themes. It is important to note, however, that I am an adult targeting an adult audience. Basically, my main idea of SFW is having a site you can safely browse at public spaces, not a kid-friendly site.

The site is not 100% accessible, even though I am constantly trying to improve it, and uses a bit of JavaScript. Please only browse it if you are okay with that.
