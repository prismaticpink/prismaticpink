Self-Hosted Webring

About

Self-Hosted Webring is a webring for adults who own sites with a custom domain and host. The indie web has some free hosts with social features that help sites stay connected, but those who go through a more DIY path had no community. That's what this webring is for!

Last update: 08/11/2025

Rules

  • You must have a domain and a host with no social features. Sorry, Neocities and Nekoweb sites do not fit this criterion, even if the social features are disabled. There are webrings exclusively for Neocities users and Nekoweb users, but no one for self-hosted sites, and that's why I created this one. No hard feelings!
  • You must be at least 18 years old to join.
  • Please specify if your site has mature/NSFW content. Any content is fine, as long as it is not illegal or hateful.
  • Once approved, grab a code and join us!
