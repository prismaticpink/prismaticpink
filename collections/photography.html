Photography

I love photography, even though I have never studied it. The main reason behind it, sadly, is the cost of cameras and photography courses. Film cameras have also considerably declined in popularity in Brazil: it's almost impossible to find places to develop films. Nonetheless, I have cultivated this hobby with my phone and the few cameras I have.

I currently own two cameras: a Kodak EasyShare C180 and a Fujifilm Instax Mini 8. They were both birthday gifts from my parents! My dad also gave me his old Yashica, it's in a box with plenty of other stuff I still need to figure out a way to send to Europe. lol
